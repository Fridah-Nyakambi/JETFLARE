Jetflare is a private Ambulance service provider operating in Kenya.

We have established a huge network of ambulances over the years to ensure that qualitu Emergency Medical Services are availabe to everyone in need. Our Ambulances are available in Nairobi, Nakuru, Kisumu and Mombasa. We strive to ensure that our ambulances reach the patient/customer within the golden 10 minutes upone receipt of a distress call. Our ambulances reach come staffed with professional and experienced team of paramedics and Emergency medical Technicians (EMTs) and duly equipped to handle all forms of medical emergencies.

Jetflare provides professional and timely EMT services all over Kenya.

events medical coverage

We offer standby ambulances to corporate and social events such as team buildings, burials, weddings ,graduations among others. The ambulances come manned by two paramedics and first aiders sufficiently trained to cater to any medical emergency.

advanced lifestyle support

Our ALS ambulances are staffed with the highest trained professionals who sufficiently equiped with the latest technology of patient monitoring. The ALS units provide the highest level of pre-hospital care in any emergency or ducing inter-facility transfer.

basic life support (BLS)

Our BLS units are staffed with certified Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs) who are trained by leading training institutions on handling emergency and non-emergency work.
